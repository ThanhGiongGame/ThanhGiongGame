## Cần sửa

| **Core Problem Statement**         | Đã khá ổn, nhưng nên đổi “open-source backend applications” thành cụm chặt hơn                                       | Sửa nhẹ                                                    |

D1 hiện vẫn có topic cũ và đoạn Business Context đang nói “controlled backend application prototypes”, dù sau đó đã nhắc open-source backend implementations. 

### D1 nên thay bằng hướng này

Topic:

Evaluating Runtime and Development Overhead of Clean Architecture Compared with Traditional Layered Architecture in Open-Source Backend Systems

Phần **3.3 Ensuring a Fair Comparison** nên sửa vì hiện đang nói hai prototype phải cùng domain rules, use cases, database schema, API endpoints, technology stack, dataset, workload. Câu đó hợp với nghiên cứu tự build prototype, nhưng lệch với open-source systems. 

Traditional Layered Architecture is used as a reference baseline, not as an assumption that it is universally superior. Because this study focuses on selected open-source backend systems, the comparison must focus on comparable architectural characteristics rather than identical business domains. The selected systems should expose identifiable architectural structures, such as controller/API layers, service or use-case layers, domain logic, repository or data-access layers, and infrastructure components. Without these controls, the results may reflect differences in domain, framework, or implementation quality rather than architectural effects.

Core Problem Statement hiện đã gần đúng vì có “open-source backend applications”, nhưng nên đổi thành “selected open-source backend systems with identifiable architectural structures” cho khớp Phase 2.
